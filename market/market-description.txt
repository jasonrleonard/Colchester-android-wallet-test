With this app you always have your colchestercoins with you, in your pocket! You can
send payments simply by scanning a QR-code or by touching two phones together
(NFC). colchestercoin Wallet is designed to be easy to use and reliable, while also
being secure and fast.

FEATURES:

• No cloud server or web service needed! This wallet is truly peer to peer.
• Display of wallet balance in colchestercoin and various other currencies.
• Sending and receiving of colchestercoin via NFC, QR-codes or colchestercoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used colchestercoin addresses.
• Enter transactions while offline, will be executed when online.
• Manages blockchain on your device (for enhanced security).
• System notifications for colchestercoin connectivity and received coins.
• App widget for colchestercoin balance (requirement: app moved to phone).

KNOWN ISSUES, PLEASE READ:

• When run for the first time, it can happen that the app shows only a white display for some seconds. This is normal.

More Information:

This app is based upon the excellent work done by the Bitcoin Wallet developers:
https://code.google.com/p/bitcoin-wallet/

It used a masterfully converted library called colchestercoinj, which was converted
from bitcoinj:
https://code.google.com/p/colchestercoinj/

You can find more about colchestercoin at
http://colchestercoin.org

If you want to contribute to colchestercoin Wallet, the project is located at
http://www.github.com/hank/colchestercoin-wallet
